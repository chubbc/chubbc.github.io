I'm currently a PhD student, supervised by A/Prof. Steven T. Flammia and Dr. Marco Tomamichel, in the Centre for Engineered Quantum Systems (EQuS), at the The University of Sydney.

I am primarily interested in the interface between quantum information principles, and the theory of quantum many body systems. Specifically I am interested in quantum Hamiltonian complexity, as well as numerical methods for simulating quantum systems, such as tensor network methods. I have also done some work on Quantum Shannon Theory, specifically using small and moderate deviation techniques.
