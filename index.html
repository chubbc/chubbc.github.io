Mathematical physicist and quantum information theorist (big pile of qubits thinking about other qubits).

I am currently a postdoc in ETH Zurich, was previously a postdoc at Université de Sherbrooke, and did my PhD and BSc at University of Sydney. My research broadly sits in four buckets:
  • QEC: Quantum error correction, with particular focus on tensor network decoding and the relationship between quantum codes and classical statistical mechanical systems
  • Fin: Finite-size effects in resource theories, specifically looking at finite-size corrections to asymptotic analyses of the theories of quantum thermodynamics and entanglement
  • CM: The interface between quantum information theory and condensed matter, with particular focus on tensor network methods
  • Misc: Various miscellaneous topics in Quantum Information
